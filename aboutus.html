About Us

About the Clinic

Aesthetic Skin Clinic is one of the leaders of Aesthetic Clinics in Rawalpindi since 1994. With huge popularity and public demand, the Islamabad Clinic was opened in 1999, which was later temporarily merged with La Chirurgie Cosmetic Laser Center in Blue Area. Now the Clinic is again an independent organization in a new and larger premises in a house in E-7 Islamabad, offering more privacy and better parking space to its clients.

Aesthetics is both an art and science. Aesthetic Skin Clinic offers a thorough treatment for the skin and body. Aesthetic Skin Clinic is known as an exclusive aesthetic clinic of sophisticated customers, which combines mastery and service.

Who is Dr. Ruxana Kazi MD, PHD?

Dr. Ruxana Kazi is the Aesthetic Director of the Clinic.

Having graduated from the University of Punjab in 1985, she received extensive training and experience in Dermatology from the Pakistan Institute of Medical Sciences.

She received her Doctorate in Cosmetic Dermatology from the USA, in 2004. She has received training in usage of lasers and skills in advanced Botox, Filler and Mesotherapy techniques from Singapore and Paris, France. She is practicing aesthetic medicine since 1994.

Dr. Kazi is a Member of the International Academy of Cosmetic Dermatology, American Academy of Aesthetic Medicine and the International Association of Physicians in Aesthetic Medicine. She attends conferences worldwide and seeks the latest advanced treatments in aesthetic medicine.
