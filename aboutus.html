Who is Dr. Ruxana Kazi

Dr. Ruxana Kazi is the Aesthetic Director of the Clinic.

Having graduated from the University of Punjab in 1984, she received extensive training and experience in Dermatology from the Pakistan Institute of Medical Sciences.

She received her Doctorate in Cosmetic Dermatology, in 2004. She has received training in usage of lasers and skills in advanced Botox, Fillers and Mesotherapy techniques from Singapore, Paris- France and Korea.She is practicing aesthetic medicine since 1994.

Dr. Kazi is a Member of the International Academy of Cosmetic Dermatology, American Academy of Aesthetic Medicine and the International Association of Physicians in Aesthetic Medicine. She attends conferences worldwide and seeks the latest advanced treatments in aesthetic medicine.
