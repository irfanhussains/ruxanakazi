Mesotherapy

Mesotherapy is a minimally-invasive, non-surgical, natural cosmetic medical treatment, suitable for a variety of symptoms and conditions.It involves multiple injections of pharmaceutical or homeopathic medication, plant extracts and vitamins.

Mesotherapy is an effective treatment for:

  • Acne
  • Stretch marks
  • Scars
  • Lines and wrinkles
  • Skin rejuvenation
  • Hair loss
  • Cellulite
  • Excess fat
